About Emoji Place


Emoji place is an app designed to make reviewing as fast and easy as possible. Whether it's somewhere you've been a thousand times before, a new place you've never even heard of, or maybe you found a neat landmark out in the middle of nowhere, it only takes a few seconds to leave a review anywhere you go.

In the app, all you need to do is tap on a location, select the emoji that best represents how you felt or what the location offers, and then tap "Add Emoji" to leave a quick review. All reviews are completely anonymous, so don't feel bad for being honest!

The website allows you to search for different places and shows you what everyone thinks of it, whether you're curious how people feel about your favorite restaurant, or you want to check somewhere new before you go, it's quick and easy to search for anywhere you want.
